Our Skinny Slice Crust is practically cheating. You get the same great flavor, while cutting calories.

We just use a smarter portion of Hand Tossed dough to make your crust skinnier. Plus, we go easy on some meats and cheeses while delivering major flavor with sauce and veggies. Our recipes are only 250 calories or less per slice, or 300 calories or less if you create your own.

If you create your own, just make sure you only use red sauce and no more than 7 toppings (3 meat toppings max). Sorry, no doubling up on the cheese or toppings, and no drizzles or crust finishers, please. Calorie reductions are based on a thinner crust and balanced portion of toppings. Not a reduced calorie food. Just a smarter portion size.
